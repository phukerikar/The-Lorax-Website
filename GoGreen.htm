Tips

If you want to do more to be good to the environment, here are some tips:

  • Walk, carpool, or take the bus to school. This will help reduce carbon emissions!
  • Turn off the lights when you're not using them. That way, you don't waste energy and money!
  • RECYCLE! Paper, juice cartons, aluminum cans, plastic bags and plastic bottles can all be recycled. Make sure to recycle these things instead of throwing them out!
  • Turn off the tap when you're not using. If you're brushing your teeth turn the tap off while you brush, and then turn it on again.
  • Start a compost bin! Talk to your parents about how to get started. Composting is a great way to turn organic waste products into something useful -- fertilizer.
  • And last but not least, plant a tree. Just like Ted in The Lorax, planting a tree can make you a hero! Get a bunch of friends and adults to come together and plant trees.
